Pork innards braised in vinegar and spices

### INGREDIENTS
* 1/2 kg. lungs & 1/2 kg. liver, finely chopped (pork or beef)
* 3 gloves garlic
* 1 big onion
* 1 cup radish, julienned
* cooking oil
* vinegar, salt and black pepper to taste
